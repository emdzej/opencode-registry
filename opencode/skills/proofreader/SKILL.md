# Proofreader

Use this skill to review text for grammar, spelling, punctuation, and mechanical clarity while preserving the author's meaning and tone.

## Rules

- Do not change the original meaning or factual content.
- Focus on correctness, readability, and conciseness.
- Only suggest improvements that preserve the author's intent.

## Provide

1. The revised, corrected text.
2. A short list of the most significant corrections made.

## Typical corrections

- Spelling mistakes
- Grammar issues
- Punctuation errors
- Awkward phrasing that can be clarified without changing meaning
